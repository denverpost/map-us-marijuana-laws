About

The interactive map shows where recreational pot use is legal, which states have legalized the medical uses of marijuana, and which ones have cannabis-related ballot initiatives in November 2016. To provide some additional context, the map includes info about when states adopted their respective regulations, whether legalization measures were voter-approved or enacted via legislation, as well as whether possession of small amounts of marijuana has been decriminalized, meaning a citation instead of possible jail time or criminal record for a first-time offense.

Instructions
  • Click a state for details about its marijuana laws
  • Double-click to zoom in on small areas, such as Washington, DC; shift + double-click to zoom out
  • To read stories about marijuana in a state, click the story archive link
Data sources: National Conference of State Legislatures, National Organization for the Reform of Marijuana Laws
